# Site Restructure Plan

## Core principle
Every product page is a ONE-STOP guide. One URL answers every question about that product. No separate pages for insurance, financing, best brands, sizing, or buyer guides.

## New page section order (per product)

1. **Quick Answer Box** — lifespan + cost at a glance
2. **How Long Does It Last** — detailed lifespan with factors
3. **Warning Signs / Signs It's Failing** — specific symptoms with product recommendations INLINE ("check your anode rod with a **Camco Magnesium Anode Rod** ($20-35)")
4. **Should You Repair or Replace** — decision framework with repair costs
5. **Common Repairs and What They Cost** — NEW section, targets "[thing] repair cost" keywords
6. **Cost to Replace** — full replacement pricing
7. **Does Insurance Cover This?** — NEW section, targets "does homeowners insurance cover [thing]" keywords
8. **Financing Options** — NEW section (for big ticket items only: roof, HVAC, windows, siding)
9. **What Size Do You Need** — NEW section, targets "what size [thing] do I need"
10. **Best Brands** — NEW section, targets "best [thing] brands"
11. **How Long Does Installation Take** — NEW section, targets "how long to replace [thing]"
12. **How to Make It Last Longer** — maintenance tips with products INLINE
13. **Related Guides** — cross-links to troubleshoot, maintenance, and related products

## Amazon link strategy
- NO separate "What We Recommend" box at the bottom
- Products embedded NATURALLY within the text where they solve a problem
- Format: "...clean the coils annually with a **coil cleaner spray like Nu-Calgon** (~$12 on Amazon)..."
- Every product mention is a potential affiliate link
- 5-8 Amazon links per page, spread throughout the content

## Which pages get which sections

### All pages get:
- Signs it's failing (with inline products)
- Common repairs and costs
- Does insurance cover this
- Best brands
- Inline Amazon links

### Big ticket pages also get ($3K+ replacement):
- Financing options
- What size do you need
- How long does installation take
- Roof, windows, furnace, AC, siding, garage door, septic, sewer line, boiler, heat pump, ductwork

### Maintenance pages get:
- Inline Amazon product links throughout
- "What you'll need" with Amazon links (already done)

### Troubleshoot pages get:
- Inline Amazon links in the fix steps
- "Tools for this job" with Amazon links
